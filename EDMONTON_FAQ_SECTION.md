### CATEGORY 1: BASICS (What is SEO?)

**Q1: What is SEO and why does my Edmonton business need it?**
SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the practice of optimizing your website to rank higher in Google search results for keywords your customers are searching for. For Edmonton businesses, SEO is crucial because 93% of online experiences begin with a search engine. When potential customers search for services like yours, appearing in the top 3 results dramatically increases your chances of getting the call or sale. Unlike paid ads that stop when you stop paying, SEO builds organic traffic that compounds over time. Whether you're a plumber, dentist, realtor, or e-commerce store in Edmonton, SEO positions you in front of customers actively looking for what you offer.

**Q2: How is SEO different from Google Ads/PPC?**
SEO and Google Ads (PPC) are complementary but different. Google Ads shows your ad at the top of results instantly—you pay per click. SEO appears in organic (non-ad) results below ads, takes 3-6 months to show results, but you don't pay per click. Think of it like renting vs. owning: Ads are renting visibility; SEO is building owned assets. Ads stop working when you stop paying. SEO keeps working. For Edmonton businesses, the ideal approach is both: use Google Ads for immediate leads while SEO builds sustainable long-term traffic.

**Q3: What's the difference between organic SEO and local SEO?**
Organic SEO targets broader searches (like 'digital marketing services' nationally), while local SEO targets location-specific searches ('SEO services Edmonton' or 'plumber near me'). For Edmonton businesses, local SEO is often more valuable because it reaches customers actively looking for your service in your area. Local SEO includes optimizing your Google My Business profile (showing your location on the map), building local citations (directory listings), managing reviews, and creating location-specific content. We optimize both together because they work synergistically. Most Edmonton businesses see 60% of their SEO traffic from local searches.

### CATEGORY 2: TIMELINE & RESULTS

**Q4: How long does SEO take in Edmonton?**
SEO is a marathon, not a sprint. Expect: Months 1-2: Audit and strategy phase—minimal traffic change, but we're laying foundation. Months 3-4: First improvements visible—ranking positions climb, organic traffic increases slightly. Months 6-12: Significant results—top keywords reach first page, traffic grows noticeably, leads increase. 12+ months: Dominating your space—top 3 rankings, consistent lead flow, compounding growth. Why the wait? Google needs time to crawl your site, recognize improvements, test rankings, and build trust. Competitive keywords take longer. But once you hit that sweet spot (usually 6+ months), you'll see consistent growth month after month.

**Q5: Can you guarantee #1 rankings?**
No—and be suspicious of agencies that claim otherwise. Google owns the algorithm, and nobody can guarantee specific rankings. What we CAN guarantee: white-hat SEO methodology, transparent monthly reporting, honest communication, and results-focused optimization toward your business goals. We've gotten hundreds of Edmonton businesses to page 1, many to top 3. But we do it the right way, following Google's guidelines. Agencies that guarantee rankings often use risky black-hat tactics that can get you penalized or banned from Google. We'd rather deliver sustainable results you can count on for years.

**Q6: What if I don't see results in 3 months?**
After 3 months, we conduct a comprehensive assessment: we review ranking improvements, traffic changes, and technical fixes implemented. If we're behind expectations, we diagnose why: Is the market highly competitive? Is the domain new? Are we targeting the right keywords? We adjust strategy, increase content efforts, refine our technical approach, or pivot keywords if needed. Sometimes patience is needed (very competitive markets take 6+ months), and sometimes strategy needs tweaking. We're transparent about realistic expectations for your specific market and keep communication open.

**Q7: How long does it take to see leads from SEO?**
Timeline for lead generation: Months 1-2: Minimal traffic, few or no leads (foundation phase). Months 3-4: Modest traffic, first leads trickling in. Months 5-6: More significant traffic, consistent lead flow. Months 6-12: Strong lead volume, predictable conversion pattern. As an example: a plumber might get 10-15 leads/month by month 4, climbing to 50-100+/month by month 8-12. Lead quality also improves over time—early traffic can include tire-kickers, but as you rank for more specific keywords, you get warmer, more qualified leads. This is why SEO is valuable: you get more AND better quality leads without paying per lead.
