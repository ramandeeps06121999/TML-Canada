## SECTION 2: WHAT IS SEO? (500 words)

### H2: What is SEO and Why Does Your Edmonton Business Need It?

**SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization.** It's the practice of optimizing your website so that search engines like Google rank it higher for keywords your customers are searching for.

Here's the simple version: When someone in Edmonton searches for "SEO services" or "plumber near me" or "dental clinic," Google shows them a list of results. The top 3 results get about 75% of all clicks. If you're not in that top 3, you're essentially invisible.

SEO gets you into that top 3. Sustainably. Permanently. Without paying per click.

### How SEO Works (Simple Version)

Google has a massive index of websites. Its algorithm analyzes hundreds of ranking factors to decide which sites deserve to be at the top for any given search. The algorithm asks: "Is this site relevant to what the searcher wants? Is it trustworthy? Is it authoritative? Will the searcher have a good experience here?"

Our job is to optimize your website on all these factors - technical quality, content relevance, user experience, authority signals, and local credibility. Once Google sees that your site is the best answer for your customers' searches, it ranks you higher.

**That's SEO.**

### SEO vs. Google Ads (PPC) - What's the Difference?

**Google Ads (PPC):**
- Your ad appears at the TOP of search results immediately
- You pay every time someone clicks your ad
- Stops working the moment you stop paying
- Good for: immediate leads, time-sensitive offers, testing keywords

**SEO (Organic Search):**
- Your result appears BELOW the ads in organic results
- Takes 3-6 months to show results
- You DON'T pay per click - you pay one monthly fee
- Keeps generating leads month after month
- Better for: long-term growth, sustainable marketing, cost-efficiency

**The Truth:** You need BOTH. Use Google Ads while SEO is building. Once SEO kicks in (month 6+), you reduce ad spend and let organic traffic do the heavy lifting.

### Why Local SEO Matters for Edmonton Businesses

**46% of all Google searches have local intent.** People search "near me," "Edmonton," or your service + location. When someone searches "emergency plumber Edmonton," they're ready to call NOW. They're not browsing - they're buying.

Local SEO gets you in front of these ready-to-buy customers at the exact moment they're searching. This is why local SEO often produces faster ROI than broad organic SEO.

Local SEO includes:
- **Google My Business optimization** (the map listing showing your location)
- **Local citations** (directory listings confirming your business exists)
- **Reviews management** (building social proof and trust)
- **Local keywords** (targeting "Edmonton," neighborhoods, "near me" searches)
- **Local content** (pages that answer Edmonton-specific questions)
