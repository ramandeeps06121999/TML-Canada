# Edmonton SEO Case Studies: 5 Detailed Success Stories

## CASE STUDY 1: LOCAL SERVICE BUSINESS
**Apex Plumbing Solutions - Emergency Plumbing & HVAC Services**

### THE CHALLENGE
Apex Plumbing Solutions, a well-established 15-year-old family-owned plumbing and HVAC company with excellent customer reviews, was virtually invisible on Google. When homeowners searched for emergency plumbing at 1 AM, Apex didn't appear. Owner Derek Mitchell watched potential customers call competitors while his schedule had empty slots.

### BEFORE & AFTER METRICS

| Metric | BEFORE | AFTER (6 months) | Change |
|--------|--------|------------------|---------|
| "Plumber Edmonton" Ranking | #45 | #3 | +42 positions |
| "Emergency Plumber Edmonton" Ranking | Not ranking | #2 | To page 1 |
| "24-Hour Plumbing Edmonton" Ranking | Not ranking | #5 | To page 1 |
| Monthly Organic Traffic | 120 visits | 2,400 visits | 2000% increase |
| Monthly Qualified Leads | 2-3 | 35-40 | 1200% increase |
| Google My Business Views | 50/month | 1,200/month | 2400% increase |
| Customer Reviews | 12 reviews | 67 reviews | +55 reviews |
| Average Review Rating | 4.3 stars | 4.8 stars | +0.5 stars |

### REVENUE IMPACT
- **6-Month Total**: 60 jobs × $2,125 average = **$127,500 revenue**
- **Net ROI** (after $8,200 investment): **$119,300 profit**

### KEY STRATEGIES USED
- Technical fixes (4.2s → 1.1s page speed)
- GMB profile optimization with 28 professional photos
- Local citations across 25 directories
- Emergency-focused content strategy
- Neighborhood-specific landing pages

### TESTIMONIAL
"Within six months, my phone was ringing constantly with new homeowners and property managers all over Edmonton. The SEO investment paid for itself in the first month and has added over $120,000 in revenue."

— Derek Mitchell, Owner, Apex Plumbing Solutions
