# Industry Keyword Gap Analysis - TML Agency

**Date:** 2026-04-14
**Scope:** 42 industries (industries.json) + 22 industry pages (industryPages.json) + 39 services
**Finding:** The current keyword list has ZERO industry+service+city compound keywords. This is the single largest SEO gap on the site.

---

## Executive Summary

TML has 42 industry entries and 22 dedicated industry pages, but the keyword strategy treats industries and services as separate silos. The site is missing thousands of low-competition, high-conversion compound keywords like "restaurant web design Toronto" or "law firm SEO Calgary." These keywords typically have 10-480 monthly searches individually but aggregate into massive traffic when combined across 42 industries x 10+ services x 150+ cities.

**Estimated total missed opportunity:** 50,000-120,000 monthly searches across all industry+service combinations in Canada.

---

## Priority Tier System

- **Tier 1 (CRITICAL):** Industries with highest digital marketing spend in Canada. Need dedicated landing pages for every service+industry combo AND city variants.
- **Tier 2 (HIGH):** Strong search demand, clear buyer intent. Need dedicated industry-service pages.
- **Tier 3 (MEDIUM):** Moderate search volume. Can start with blog content and sections on existing industry pages.
- **Tier 4 (NICHE):** Lower volume but very low competition. Blog posts and long-tail content.
