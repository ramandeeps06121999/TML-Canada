## Optimization Notes

1. **Keyword cannibalization risk**: The homepage targets multiple broad "agency/company" variants. Ensure internal linking clearly differentiates homepage (brand/agency) from `/services/seo` (service-specific) intent.

2. **"Near me" keywords**: `marketing agency near me` maps to the homepage but requires strong local SEO signals -- Google Business Profile optimization, consistent NAP across citations, LocalBusiness schema markup, and city-specific content on the homepage.

3. **"Canada" modifier keywords** (seo canada, seo agency canada, seo company canada, seo services canada): All map to `/services/seo`. Ensure the page includes Canada-focused content, mentions serving clients nationwide, and uses "Canada" in H2 subheadings or a dedicated section.

4. **"Best" modifier keywords** (best seo company): Maps to `/services/seo` as the primary target. Consider creating a supporting blog post like "Best SEO Companies in Canada (2026)" that links to the service page -- this captures informational intent while the service page captures transactional intent.

5. **Toronto cluster** has 6 keywords and 12,900 combined volume, making it the highest-value location-service page. Prioritize content depth and link building for `/services/seo-in-toronto`.

6. **Calgary cluster** has 4 keywords and 6,700 combined volume -- second-highest location. `/services/seo-in-calgary` should be a priority for optimization.

7. **"digital marketing agency toronto"** (1,900 vol) currently maps to the Toronto SEO page. Consider whether a dedicated Toronto location hub page (`/locations/toronto` or similar) would better serve this broader intent. Currently the location-service pages are the closest match.

8. **Suggested new blog content** to support these keywords:
   - "Best SEO Companies in Canada (2026)" -- supports "best seo company" and "seo company canada"
   - "SEO Consultant vs SEO Agency: Which Do You Need?" -- supports "seo consultant" and "seo expert"
   - "Local SEO Guide for Canadian Businesses" -- supports "local seo", "local seo services", "local seo company"
