## Week 7-8: Technical Optimization

**Goal:** Build the technical infrastructure that amplifies all content efforts -- internal linking, schema, AI optimization, and page speed.

### Week 7: Internal Linking + Schema Overhaul

#### Task 7.1: Internal Linking Architecture
- **Action:** Deploy a systematic internal linking strategy across all 1,488+ location-service pages:

**Hub-and-Spoke Model:**
- **Hub pages:** 7 city guides (from Week 6) + 39 service pages
- **Spoke pages:** 1,488 location-service pages
- Each spoke links to:
  - Its city hub page
  - Its service hub page
  - 3-5 related service pages in the same city
  - 2-3 same-service pages in nearby cities

**Implementation:**
- Add "Related Services in {City}" section to each location-service page (links to 5 other services in same city)
- Add "This Service in Nearby Cities" section (links to 3-5 geographic neighbors)
- Add contextual links within body content pointing to blog posts
- Add breadcrumb links: Home > {City} > {Service}
- Ensure every new blog post links to 5+ relevant location-service pages

- **Deliverable:** Internal linking template deployed across all pages; link audit showing average internal links per page increased from current baseline to 8-12
- **Priority:** P0
- **Owner:** Web Developer + SEO Strategist
- **Hours:** 20

**Location-Service Pages (1,488 pages):**
