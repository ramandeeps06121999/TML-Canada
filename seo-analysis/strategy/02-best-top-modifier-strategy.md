## Content Guidelines for All "Best/Top" Listicle Pages

### Credibility requirements (critical for ranking)
- List at least 8-15 companies per listicle -- never just TML alone
- Include genuine pros AND cons for every company including TML
- Cite verifiable information: founding year, team size, notable clients, review scores
- Link to each company's website
- Include a transparent methodology section explaining ranking criteria

### On-page SEO
- Title tag format: "{Primary Keyword} ({Year}) | TML Digital"
- Meta description: Include primary keyword + CTA (e.g., "See which agencies made our list")
- H1: Match or closely mirror the title tag
- H2s: Use keyword variants (e.g., "Best SEO Agency", "Top SEO Company", "Top Rated SEO Firms")
- Image alt text: "[Company Name] - [service] agency" for company logos/screenshots

### Schema markup (apply to every listicle)
- **Article** schema (author, datePublished, dateModified)
- **FAQ** schema for the FAQ section (5+ questions)
- **ItemList** schema listing each company entry
- Update `dateModified` whenever the page is refreshed (at minimum quarterly)

### Conversion elements
- Sticky CTA sidebar: "Get a Free Audit from TML"
- In-content CTA after TML entry: "Ready to work with one of Canada's best? Get started"
- Comparison table at the top for quick scanners
- Exit-intent popup offering a free consultation

### Freshness strategy
- Update all listicle pages every 6 months with new data, pricing changes, and company updates
- Change the year in the title tag annually (2026 -> 2027)
- Add a "Last updated: [date]" notice at the top of each page
- Refreshing content signals relevance to Google and maintains rankings

## Risk Mitigation

1. **Thin content risk:** Each page must have genuine, researched competitor entries -- not filler. Google devalues "best of" pages that are thinly disguised self-promotion.

2. **Cannibalization with service pages:** The blog listicles target informational/comparison intent ("which is the best?") while service pages target transactional intent ("hire an SEO company"). Keep the intent distinction clear. Listicles should funnel readers TO service pages, not compete with them.

3. **Review/update burden:** 8 listicle pages need refreshing every 6 months. Build a content calendar reminder. Stale "Best of 2024" pages lose rankings quickly.

4. **Competitor reaction:** Other agencies create similar listicles. Differentiate with deeper research, real pricing data, and genuine pros/cons that competing self-promotional lists lack.
