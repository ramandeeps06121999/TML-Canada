## SECTION 17: RED FLAGS & CAVEATS

### DATA QUALITY ISSUES TO WATCH

1. **Business Count Decline (2024-2025: -4.14%)**
   - Conflicting with growth narrative
   - May reflect business consolidation, not market decline
   - Consider investigating before emphasizing
   - Use with context: "Despite market consolidation, opportunities abound..."

2. **Business Confidence Decline**
   - Statistics Canada Q2 2025 showing lowest since Q1 2024
   - May indicate economic headwinds
   - Balance with optimism: "47% still expect revenue growth despite challenges"
   - Frame SEO as recession-resistant investment

3. **Real Estate Market Cooling**
   - -11.5% YoY sales decline (Feb 2026)
   - Conflicting with strong monthly activity (+39.7% MoM)
   - Likely seasonal; watch for trend confirmation
   - Use cautiously for real estate marketing

4. **Weather Dependency Risk**
   - 2024 warm winter reduced snow removal demand 50%
   - Climate unpredictability affects seasonal planning
   - Recommend year-round revenue diversification messaging

5. **Outdated Income Data**
   - Most recent income data is 2023 (1-3 years old)
   - Use for general context, not precise purchasing power
   - Consider annual inflation (~2%) when extrapolating

6. **Limited Canada-Specific Digital Data**
   - Most voice search, local search data is US-based
   - Apply to Canada with caution; may overstate adoption
   - Look for Canadian-specific sources when available
