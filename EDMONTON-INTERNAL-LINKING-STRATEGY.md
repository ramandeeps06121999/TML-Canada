# Edmonton SEO Page - Internal Linking Strategy

## Executive Summary

- **Current Links**: ~6 implemented
- **Recommended Additions**: 16-18 new strategic links
- **Target Total**: 20-22 links (optimal density: 1 per 320-400 words)
- **Expected Impact**: +15-25% internal traffic, +2-5 ranking positions, +8-15% consultation requests

---

## HIGH PRIORITY LINKS (Implement First)

### 1. Case Studies Validation
- **Anchor**: "See our success stories" / "500+ successful projects"
- **Target**: `/case-studies`
- **Location**: Why Choose Us + Trust sections
- **Impact**: HIGH - Credibility proof

### 2. Industries Hub (6-8 Links)
- **Anchors**: Real Estate, Healthcare, E-commerce, Trades, etc.
- **Target**: `/industries/[industry-slug]`
- **Location**: "Industries We Serve" section
- **Impact**: HIGH - 25-40% traffic to industry pages

### 3. Service Components (4-6 Links)
- **Anchors**: Keyword Research, On-Page SEO, Link Building, Technical SEO, Content, Local SEO
- **Target**: `/services/[service-slug]`
- **Location**: "Services Offered" section
- **Impact**: HIGH - Service cluster authority

### 4. Free Consultation CTA
- **Anchor**: "Free SEO consultation" / "Schedule consultation"
- **Target**: `/contact`
- **Location**: Process section + FAQ + CTA sections
- **Impact**: HIGH - Early/late funnel conversions

### 5. Industry-Specific Services (6-8 Links)
- **Anchors**: "Real Estate SEO", "E-commerce SEO", "Healthcare SEO", etc.
- **Target**: `/services/seo-for-[industry]-edmonton/`
- **Location**: Industry service blocks
- **Impact**: HIGH - Vertical + horizontal clustering
