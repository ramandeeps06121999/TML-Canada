## WHAT IS THIS STRATEGY?

The hub-and-spoke model treats your Edmonton SEO page as the **central hub** connected to three **topical content clusters** via strategic internal links. Like spokes on a wheel, each cluster reinforces authority back to the main hub.

### The 3 Clusters:

1. **LOCAL SEO HUB** — Google My Business, citations, reviews
2. **TECHNICAL SEO HUB** — Site speed, mobile, Core Web Vitals
3. **CONTENT MARKETING HUB** — SEO writing, blog strategy, keyword research

Each cluster contains:
- A pillar page (hub of each cluster)
- 3-4 spoke pages (supporting content)
- Blog posts that link back to main page

---

## WHY THIS WORKS

### For Google:
- **Topic Authority:** Demonstrates deep expertise in SEO (not just location)
- **Content Quality:** Multiple supporting pages validate main page credibility
- **Crawlability:** Clear navigation paths through content clusters
- **Relevance:** Internal links help Google understand content relationships

### For Users:
- **Education:** Users discover related services while learning
- **Guided Journey:** Links create natural conversion funnel
- **Trust:** Comprehensive content = perceived authority
- **Multiple Entry Points:** Can enter at blog post → discover services

### For Conversions:
- **Lower Bounce Rate:** Users stay longer, explore more
- **Higher Engagement:** Multiple clicks = more invested prospect
- **Better Warm-Up:** Educational content before sales pitch
- **Multiple Paths:** Blog, case studies, services, pricing all interlinked

## ANCHOR TEXT STRATEGY

Keep it natural and diverse:

- **Primary Keywords (40%):** "SEO in Edmonton," "Edmonton SEO services"
- **Long-tail (30%):** "SEO agency Edmonton," "Edmonton SEO specialist"
- **Brand (15%):** "Our Services," "Learn about our SEO"
- **Generic (15%):** "Learn more," "View services," "Click here"

**Why this matters:** Avoid over-optimization penalty. Google prefers varied, natural anchor text.

## CRITICAL SUCCESS FACTORS

### 1. NATURAL INTEGRATION
Links must feel helpful, not forced. Example:

❌ "We do SEO in Edmonton and offer local SEO"
✅ "For Edmonton businesses competing in local search, our local SEO services drive 300%+ traffic gains"

### 2. ANCHOR TEXT DIVERSITY
Don't use the same anchor for every link.

✅ Mix: "SEO in Edmonton," "our services," "learn more," "Edmonton SEO," etc.

### 3. USER EXPERIENCE FIRST
Ask: "Will this link help the user?" before adding it.

### 4. COMPREHENSIVE TESTING
Test all links before publishing. One broken link damages credibility.

### 5. ONGOING MONITORING
Review monthly:
- Rankings (target keywords)
- Traffic trends
- Conversion rate
- Broken links
- New linking opportunities
