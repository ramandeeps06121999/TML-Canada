# TML Agency — Blog Keyword Strategy (Canada)

**Date:** 2026-04-21
**Status:** Design approved, pending spec review
**Author:** Brainstorming session (TML Agency + Claude)

## Problem

TML Agency (townmedialabs.ca) has 74 published blogs and is not receiving meaningful organic traffic or qualified leads. The current blog mix is topically scattered — e.g. "how to create an AI influencer" and "how to promote a song on Spotify" pull consumer readers who will never hire a full-service marketing agency. Meanwhile, the site is thin on the commercial, Canadian-market keywords that would bring actual buyers.

## Goal

Build a blog content strategy that generates BOTH:

1. Organic traffic volume (brand awareness, DA growth, backlinks)
2. Qualified agency leads (Canadian business owners actively in-market for branding, web dev, SEO, Google Ads, social media, lead generation, graphic design, and video services)

## Scope

- **In scope:** Blog keyword strategy, content pillar definition, publishing cadence, keyword quality gates, first 90-day roadmap, live DataForSEO validation.
- **Out of scope:** On-page SEO rewrites of existing 74 blogs, technical SEO fixes, link-building strategy, paid media, social media content calendar. These are separate tracks and will be spec'd separately if needed.

## Constraints

- **Target market:** Canadian SERPs only (lower competition, winnable).
- **Capacity:** 10+ blogs per month (aggressive publishing, AI-assisted or team writing).
- **Data-driven:** Keyword selections must be validated against live DataForSEO numbers (search volume, KD) before committing writing effort.
- **Existing inventory:** 74 blogs already published. New keywords must not cannibalize existing ranked content.

## Strategy — Hybrid Pillar Model

Three parallel content tracks, each with a distinct purpose.

### Pillar 1 — Traffic Magnets (40% of output, ~5/month at 12/month cadence)

Broad Canadian-searched informational keywords. Builds DA, brand recall, and backlinks. Will not directly convert but feeds the flywheel.

**Themes:**

- AI marketing — "AI marketing tools 2026", "ChatGPT for small business", "AI content generators Canada"
- Platform trends — "Instagram Reels trends 2026", "TikTok algorithm Canada", "YouTube Shorts strategy"
- Free tools / templates — "free logo makers Canada", "Canva alternatives", "free SEO tools 2026"
- DIY how-to guides — "how to rank on Google Canada", "how to run Facebook ads 2026", "how to build a Shopify store"
- Canadian data & trends — "Canadian ecommerce statistics 2026", "Canada digital ad spend", "Alberta small business statistics"

### Pillar 2 — Commercial Bullseye (40% of output, ~5/month at 12/month cadence)

Buyer-intent keywords. Lower volume, significantly higher conversion. These are the posts that turn blog traffic into leads.

**Themes:**

- Cost / pricing — "SEO cost Canada", "web design pricing Toronto", "how much does Google Ads cost Canada", "branding cost small business Canada"
- Agency comparisons — "best SEO agencies Calgary", "top branding agencies Toronto", "marketing agencies Edmonton reviewed"
- Service comparisons — "Google Ads vs Meta Ads Canada", "SEO vs Google Ads ROI", "agency vs in-house marketing Canada"
- Hiring guides — "how to hire a marketing agency Canada", "questions to ask SEO agency", "agency red flags"
- ROI / timeline — "SEO timeline Canada", "when will SEO show results", "Google Ads ROI expectations"

### Pillar 3 — Industry Verticals (20% of output, ~2/month at 12/month cadence)

Canadian industries crossed with TML's core services. Lowest volume per post but highest per-reader conversion rate.

**Target verticals (highest LTV × local search volume):**

1. Dentists & dental clinics
2. Law firms
3. Real estate agents
4. Home services (HVAC, plumbers, electricians)
5. Healthcare clinics (physiotherapy, chiropractic, massage)
6. Immigration consultants (large vertical in Canada, low competition)
7. Restaurants & cafes

## Cadence & 90-Day Roadmap

**Monthly output:** 12 blogs/month (3/week).

**Steady-state split:** 5 Traffic + 5 Commercial + 2 Vertical.

**First 90 days — "quick wins first" sequencing.** Hit low-KD commercial and vertical keywords first for the fastest ROI. Traffic magnets come later once DA has grown.

| Month | Traffic | Commercial | Vertical | Rationale |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Month 1** | 10% (1) | 60% (7) | 30% (4) | Commercial/vertical have lowest competition in the niche. Fastest revenue path. |
| **Month 2** | 30% (4) | 40% (5) | 30% (3) | DA has grown enough to start ranking traffic posts. |
| **Month 3+** | 40% (5) | 40% (5) | 20% (2) | Steady state. Balanced flywheel. |

**Per-post mechanics (applies to every blog):**

- Internal links: 2-3 to related service pages, 1 to a relevant location page.
- FAQ section at bottom for AI Overview / featured snippet eligibility.
- Schema: Article + FAQPage.
- Republish/refresh top 10 blogs every 6 months.

**YAGNI rule:** No keyword with KD >30 in Canada gets written in the first 90 days — regardless of how strategically "important" the topic feels.

## Keyword Quality Gates

Every keyword considered must pass all hard gates before it makes the calendar.

### Hard gates

| Criterion | Pillar 1 (Traffic) | Pillar 2 (Commercial) | Pillar 3 (Vertical) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Canada monthly search volume | ≥300 | ≥50 | ≥20 |
| Keyword Difficulty (KD) | ≤30 | ≤25 | ≤20 |
| SERP intent match | Informational | Commercial / Transactional | Commercial |
| Weak sites in top 10 | ≥2 with DA <40 | ≥3 with DA <50 | ≥4 with DA <40 |

**Why volume floors differ:** A "SEO cost Canada" query at 100/mo is worth ~10x a "marketing tips" query at 5,000/mo in lead value. Vertical posts at 20/mo = 240 highly-targeted buyers per year.

### Soft signals (nice-to-have)

- Trending up in Google Trends (not a declining topic).
- "People Also Ask" present (we'll answer as FAQ).
- Written content dominating the SERP (not Reddit / YouTube / Shopping).
- Canadian commercial intent (.ca results, Canadian agencies ranking).

### Disqualifiers (auto-reject)

- YMYL topics we're not qualified on (medical, legal advice).
- SERP dominated by shopping carousels or product results.
- Competitor brand keywords.
- Already ranked by TML (cannibalization risk).

### Data sources for validation

- **DataForSEO Labs API** — Canada search volume, KD, CPC, intent.
- **DataForSEO SERP API** — top-10 DA check for competition assessment.
- **Google Trends** — momentum and seasonality check.
- **Internal** — cross-reference against current ranked pages in Google Search Console (avoid cannibalization).

## Success Criteria

Measured at 90 days and 180 days from the date the first blog from this strategy is published.

| Metric | 90 days | 180 days |
|---|---|---|
| New blogs published | 36 | 72 |
| Blogs ranking in top 20 (Canada) | ≥12 | ≥40 |
| Blogs ranking in top 10 (Canada) | ≥4 | ≥20 |
| Monthly organic sessions from new blogs | ≥500 | ≥3,000 |
| Qualified agency leads from blog CTAs | ≥5 | ≥25 |
| Featured snippets / AI Overviews captured | ≥2 | ≥10 |
