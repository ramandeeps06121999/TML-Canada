## What "Unique Content" Means per Page

Each page gets **100% unique**:

### 1. Meta Title (45-55 chars, with power words)
Formula: `{Power Word} {Service} {Type} in {City} | {Differentiator}`

Power word examples:
- "Best {Service} Agency in {City}"
- "#1 {Service} Company {City}"
- "Top-Rated {Service} Experts {City}"
- "Award-Winning {Service} {City}"
- "{City}'s #1 {Service} Company"
- "Leading {Service} Agency {City}"
- "Expert {Service} Services {City}"

### 2. Meta Description (150-160 chars, unique CTA)
Include: primary keyword + city + 1 unique benefit + CTA

### 3. H1 (Unique per page)
- "Best Google Ads Company in Edmonton"
- "#1 Lead Generation Company in Edmonton"
- "Edmonton's Top Web Design Agency"

### 4. Hero Tagline (Unique per page)
- 1 sentence, city-specific, keyword-rich

### 5. Hero Description (Unique per page)
- 2-3 sentences mentioning city-specific context

### 6. Why Choose Us (4 unique reasons per page)
- Not template copy — reference actual city landmarks, industries
- Example for Edmonton: mention Oil Sands, Whyte Ave, University of Alberta
- Example for Toronto: mention Bay Street, Distillery District, U of T

### 7. Process Steps (4 unique descriptions per page)
- Each step's description references the city's market specifics

### 8. Local Content (3 paragraphs × 100 words = 300+ words)
- Paragraph 1: City-specific market overview
- Paragraph 2: Industry-specific insights for that city
- Paragraph 3: Why TML for this specific market

### 9. Market Insight (Unique stat + headline + body)
- Real or plausible city-specific data point
- 3-sentence explanation

### 10. FAQs (5-8 unique Q&As per page)
- Specific to city + service combination
- Include city name in 2-3 answers
- Include pricing range
- Include timeline

### 11. Case Studies/Testimonials (2-3 unique per page)
- City-based client examples
