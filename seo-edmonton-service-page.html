What Is SEO? (And Why Every Edmonton Business Needs It)

Let's start with the basics. SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization. It's the practice of improving your website so it ranks higher on Google, Bing, and other search engines for keywords that matter to your business.

Think of it this way: Google's job is to connect people searching for solutions with businesses that have those solutions. Your job is to prove to Google that your business is the best solution. SEO is how you do that.

The Three Pillars of SEO

  • Technical SEO: Making sure your website's foundation is solid—fast loading speeds, mobile-friendly design, proper code structure, and secure connections.
  • On-Page Optimization: Crafting content that answers what customers are searching for, with proper headers, keywords used naturally, and clear calls-to-action.
  • Authority & Trust: Building backlinks from reputable websites, getting positive reviews, and establishing your business as a credible expert in your field.

SEO vs. Google Ads: Which Should You Use?

This is a question we hear constantly, and the answer is: both, but let's be clear about the difference.

Google Ads (PPC) is like paying for visibility. You bid on keywords, and when someone searches, your ad shows up at the top. You pay every time someone clicks. It's fast—you can get traffic tomorrow. But it's expensive, and the moment you stop paying, the clicks stop.

SEO (Organic Search) is like earning visibility. You optimize your website, and Google naturally ranks you higher because your site is better than competitors'. It takes longer (typically 3-6 months before breakthrough results), but once you're ranked, you get free traffic month after month. A well-executed SEO strategy builds value over time.

Here's what most businesses don't realize: 65% of all clicks go to organic search results, not ads. People trust organic results more. And the ROI is dramatically better. A client paying $10,000/month for Google Ads might get 100 clicks and 5 leads. That same $10,000 invested in SEO compounds every single month—by month 6, you're getting 200+ organic clicks with zero additional cost.

The smart strategy? Use Google Ads for immediate traffic while you build your organic rankings with SEO. Once your organic rankings kick in, reduce your ad spend. This is exactly what our best clients do.
